Google Photos is bringing video editing down to "a few taps." Its new feature, Video Remix, runs on the Gemini Omni model and turns an ordinary video into a stylized, shareable clip in seconds using templates. In Google's words: "Creating beautiful video clips shouldn't require professional skills or hours of editing."

## In brief

- **What launched:** Video Remix, a new AI feature in Google Photos' "Create" tab, powered by Gemini Omni.
- **What it does:** relights dark clips cinematically, swaps backgrounds, and adds artistic effects — watercolor, sketch, oil painting — from a library of templates in a few taps.
- **Who it's for:** for now, only eligible Google AI Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers, rolling out in select countries (the list wasn't shared).
- **What it isn't:** not a model that generates video from scratch — it's an effects-and-template tool that restyles a video you already have.

## Our take

The value for you is real but limited. If you make quick, good-looking clips for social or marketing, producing a stylized video in a few taps without editing skills saves time. But there are two catches: it needs a paid Google AI subscription, and it's only in select countries — no guarantee it lands in your market soon. And set expectations: this isn't a studio that generates video from scratch; it's a tool that dresses an existing video in effects and templates. So it's a small but handy add-on that speeds content up a bit — not a big creative leap.
